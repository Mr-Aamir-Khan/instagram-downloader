Is ReelRipper completely free? ▼
Yes — 100% free. No hidden charges, no subscription, no account required.
Can I download private Instagram videos? ▼
No. Only content from public accounts can be downloaded. Private account videos are not accessible.
What video quality can I expect? ▼
We fetch the highest available quality (up to 1080p HD), matching the original upload quality on Instagram.
Do I need to install any software? ▼
No installation needed. ReelRipper works entirely in your browser on any device.
Is downloading Instagram videos legal? ▼
